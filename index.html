Summary

Our Group was commissioned by the ISFO and UCEAC to propose research with respect to advocacy, communication, and social mobilization to support institutions, which cater to marginalized sectors particularly those who are often stricken with stigma because of moral and social circumstances.

SAKADAB (Samahan ng may iba't ibang Kakayahan sa Dabaw). Are a group of physically challenged individuals living in Davao City with different acquired skills who came together from The Lady of Victory - Training Center to work towards a common goal of increasing the quality of living and becoming self-reliant. Their skills include but are not limited to baking, handicraft, woodcraft, and welding. SAKADAB is an institution focused on catering to physically challenged individuals, increasing their quality of living, become self-reliant, and live meaningful lives by providing each other spiritual, moral, and financial support.

This webpage serves to not only educate you about the institution, but to also educate you about our project detail, such as the background of the project, that contains a detailed description about the problem that S.A.K.A.D.A.B is facing. Other information that you might find in this webpage is the significance of the problem is to our community especially our Institution, how this affects everyone else around us especially the Institution, and why it is important to know and solve. Lastly, you would find about our proposed project, more information about the Institution and their situation in the M.A.P.E.H section, and Lastly Our Science Investigatory Project.
